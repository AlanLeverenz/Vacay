A POWERFUL TRAVEL COMPANION NO MATTER WHERE LIFE TAKES YOU.
Whether you are a novice or seasoned professional traveller VACAY is your critical go-to tool when travelling. Our teams mission was to create a comprehensive app that puts everything you need to know in one convenient place. There are other apps where you can book your tickets, or see the weather by location and so on. VACAY's beauty is that know you no longer need several different apps (and their passwords) to get all of your important travel information. VACAY's rich features and search engine give you everything, easily readable in one place.
OUR MAPS AND NAVIGATION WILL NOT LEAVE YOU STRANDED
Powered by Google, our maps can be used for a broad geographic overview or a highly detailed map of city streets. In addition to this flexibility you will never feel lost with our in app navigation features.
IN DEPTH CULTURAL INFORMATION.
Our search engines will return a wealth of cultural information in a variety of fields. Everything from language, population, neighboring countries and much more. We will have you feeling more like a local than a tourist with the click of a button.
BRAVE THE ELEMENTS
Never find yourself without an umbrella or a jacket. You will be well prepared for the road ahead of you before you ever set foot off of the plane. We'll meet all of your meteorological needs no matter where you go.
HOW FAR WILL YOUR DOLLAR TAKE YOU.
VACAY will provide you with the most current international exchange rates. Know what your money is worth ahead of time so that you can be prepared to get a fair exchange rate for your hard earned money.
STAYING CURRENT AND DIGGING DEEPER
VACAY will make sure that you are up to speed with all current and cultural events for your destination. As well as any other relevant articles including sports, opinion pieces and more. All brought to you from the archives of the New York Times.
STAYING ORGANIZED
It is not easy keeping all of your flights, hotels, rentals organized. That is why we have an in app itiniery creator that lets you keep all of your important times, dates and more in one convenient and trusted place.
BON VOYAGE
You can rest assured that when you travel with VACAY you are never alone. We have put a lot of time, sweat equity and heart into making VACAY the app that WE would want at our side when WE travel abroad.
